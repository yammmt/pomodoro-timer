# Quickstart

## Goal

Verify Pause/Resume works after a session completes and the timer is overdue.

## Steps

1. Start a work session and wait for it to complete (timer hits 00:00 and continues as -MM:SS).
2. Click Pause and confirm the negative time stops changing.
3. Click Resume and confirm the negative time continues from the frozen value.
4. Repeat for a break session.
